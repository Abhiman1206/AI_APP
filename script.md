# Intelli-Credit: Video Presentation Script

**Duration:** ~5 Minutes (approx. 650 words)
**Speaker Tone:** Enthusiastic, Professional, and Solution-Oriented

---

## 1. Intro: The Hook (0:00 - 0:45)

**[Visual: Montage of messy, scanned PDFs, bank statements, and a stressed credit manager.]**

**Speaker:** In the high-stakes world of Indian corporate lending, credit managers face a crushing irony. We call it the "Data Paradox." 

Institutional lenders are drowning in more data than ever—ITRs, GST filings, bank statements, and unstructured sanction letters. Yet, processing a single loan application still takes *weeks*. Why? Because the intelligence is trapped. It’s buried in messy, scanned PDFs and disparate datasets that don't talk to each other.

**[Visual: Logo of Intelli-Credit appears with the tagline "Bridging the Intelligence Gap".]**

**Speaker:** Welcome to **Intelli-Credit**. We’ve built an end-to-end, AI-powered Credit Decisioning Engine designed to turn these weeks of manual labor into minutes of high-precision insight.

---

## 2. The Problem: The Data Paradox (0:45 - 1:15)

**[Visual: Split screen showing a "Traditional Workflow" (Slow, Manual, Prone to bias) vs. "Intelli-Credit Workflow" (Fast, Automated, Precise).]**

**Speaker:** Today, credit analysts spend 80% of their time stitching data and only 20% on actual decision-making. This manual process is prone to human bias and often misses "early warning signals" like revenue inflation or circular trading, until it’s too late. Intelli-Credit fixes this by automating the heavy lifting.

---

## 3. Pillar 1: The Data Ingestor & Fraud Detection (1:15 - 2:00)

**[Visual: Screen recording showing a messy, scanned Sanction Letter being uploaded.]**

**Speaker:** It starts with our **Data Ingestor**. This isn't just basic OCR. Our pipeline uses vision-aware models to parse commitments and risks from even the messiest, scanned Indian-context PDFs, like legacy sanction letters and board minutes.

**[Visual: Close-up of "Circular Trading Flag" on the dashboard showing GST vs Bank variance.]**

**Speaker:** But here’s the magic: **Structured Synthesis**. It automatically cross-leverages GST returns directly against bank statement credits. If there's a significant disparity, Intelli-Credit instantly flags it as possible "circular trading" or revenue inflation—high-risk patterns that would take a human analyst days to detect.

---

## 4. Pillar 2: The Digital Credit Manager (2:00 - 2:45)

**[Visual: Dashboard showing autonomously crawled litigation history from e-Courts and MCA.]**

**Speaker:** Next is our **Digital Credit Manager**. It performs deep "web-scale" secondary research. Our agents autonomously crawl **e-Courts** and **MCA records** to unearth active promoter disputes and litigation history that aren't in the provided files.

**[Visual: Credit Officer entering notes in the "Primary Insight Portal": "Factory operating at 40% capacity".]**

**Speaker:** We also bridge digital data with ground reality. Through our **Primary Insight Portal**, credit officers can log qualitative observations from site visits. The AI doesn't just store these; it dynamically adjusts the final risk score based on these nuances.

---

## 5. Pillar 3: The Recommendation Engine & XAI (2:45 - 3:45)

**[Visual: A Radar Chart showing the "Five Cs of Credit" (Character, Capacity, Capital, Collateral, Conditions).]**

**Speaker:** All this intelligence flows into our **Recommendation Engine**. Firmly anchored in the **Five Cs of Credit**, our engine calculates a precise loan limit and risk premium.

**[Visual: Close-up of an "XAI Explanation" block: "Limit reduced by 15% due to high litigation risk found in secondary research."]**

**Speaker:** But we don’t believe in black boxes. Intelli-Credit features **100% Explainable AI**. It "walks you through" the rationale, explicitly linking every recommendation to specific evidence found during ingestion or research. 

**[Visual: User clicking "Download CAM" and a professional PDF report opening up.]**

**Speaker:** With one click, it generates a comprehensive, enterprise-ready **Credit Appraisal Memo**, or CAM, summarizing the entire analysis for the credit committee.

---

## 6. Technical Excellence (3:45 - 4:15)

**[Visual: Architecture diagram showing Next.js 15, FastAPI, and Databricks integration.]**

**Speaker:** Under the hood, we've built a decoupled, high-latency capable system. Utilizing **Next.js 15** and **FastAPI**, and designed to be natively **Databricks-compatible**, it can scale horizontally to process thousands of applications concurrently while maintaining military-grade security.

## 7. Conclusion: The Impact (4:15 - 5:00)

**[Visual: Shot of a Credit Manager looking confident and focused.]**

**Speaker:** Intelli-Credit doesn't just replace manual work; it elevates the human analyst. It ends the Data Paradox, eliminates bias, and uncovers hidden risks before they become NPAs.

**[Visual: Final logo and contact info.]**

**Speaker:** We are bridging the intelligence gap in corporate lending. We are **Intelli-Credit**. Thank you.
